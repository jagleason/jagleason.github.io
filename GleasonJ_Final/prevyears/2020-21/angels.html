ANGELS IN AMERICA by TPNY KUSHNER

DIRECTED BY HANNAH FATOOL and M NOTTKE

Angels in America is a story of love, loss, the human journey of self- discovery, and all the little moments of suffering and elation and feeling whole that self-discovery can bring with it.
Angels is also a story about the devastating and horrific AIDS epidemic that disproportionately affected and affects a minority group, the LGBTQ+ community, and a governemnt that is too concerned with maintaining their power and saving face to help those in need.
Angels in America was rehearsed and will be performed entirely virtually.
