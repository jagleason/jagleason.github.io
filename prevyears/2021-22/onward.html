ONWARD, CHEF DOMÍNGUEZ! by GIAN GIBBONEY

DIRECTED BY MIASOL YARA

Onward, Chef Domínguez! is a new musical written by current-UNC student Gian Gibboney that tells a story of life, love, and baking. Come follow Sofia Domínguez as she works towards her dream of becoming a pastry chef, connects to her roots, and develops her relationship with her girlfriend Riley all while battling with OCD.
Onward, Chef Domínguez! is showing at the Carrboro ArtsCenter on November 5th & 6th at 7PM and October 7th at 2:30 PM.
Proof of vaccination or a negative COVID test from the last 72 hours required to enter, a photo is acceptable. Tickets will not be refunded if proof is not provided at the door. Masks are mandatory for the entirety of the performance, eating and drinking inside the theatre is prohibited.
